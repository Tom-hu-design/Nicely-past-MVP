What is the problem that Nicely is aiming to solve?
"Decisions of care provision are made on the bases of limited data and monitoring due to significant grey zones in between appointments; this often leads to a disconnection between the care provider and client, resulting in high misdiagnosis rates and frustration from both parties regarding the level of care given."
What is Nicely's Vision?
"Our vision is to lower misdiagnosis rates for mental illnesses, provide more data-driven care for clients in between appointments, and improve the current diagnosis processes while entirely removing social stigma from the mental health field."
What is Nicely's Mission?
"Our mission is to support mental health providers to give highly individualized and data-driven care for their clients and create more impactful client/counselor relationships."
